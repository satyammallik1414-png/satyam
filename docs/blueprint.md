# **App Name**: Eternal Bloom

## Core Features:

- Responsive Multi-Page Layout: Implement a fully responsive multi-page architecture with Next.js routing, including Home, Love Letter, Memory Gallery, Reasons, Surprise, and Promise pages, ensuring smooth navigation transitions.
- Animated Hero Section & Countdown: Display a full-screen hero section on the home page with an animated background gradient, dynamically highlighted subheading, a countdown timer to the birthday, and a 'Start Surprise' button with a fade-in animation.
- Interactive Love Letter Display: Render a love letter page with a 'paper-style' aesthetic, incorporating a typewriter text effect for the message and floating glowing particles for an enchanting visual experience.
- Dynamic Memory Gallery: Showcase memories in a modern grid layout with image hover zoom effects and a lightbox preview when images are clicked, complete with captions and smooth page transitions.
- Reasons I Love You Cards: Present at least 10 animated cards, each flipping on hover to reveal a romantic reason, accompanied by heart icons and subtle glow effects for an interactive and emotional experience.
- Interactive Surprise Finale: Trigger a grand finale of animations, including a confetti explosion, an animated cake with a candle, and fireworks, upon clicking a 'Click for Final Surprise' button.
- Personalized Message & Reason Tool: A generative AI tool that suggests ideas for the 'long emotional birthday message' and '10 romantic reasons' based on themes or keywords provided by the user.

## Style Guidelines:

- The primary accent color for key elements and glowing effects is a vivid, romantic magenta (#D140B4), chosen to evoke deep affection and warmth without being overtly loud.
- A soft, barely-there background pink (#FCEEF8) sets a delicate and airy mood, derived from the primary hue but heavily desaturated to allow content to stand out elegantly.
- An enchanting violet (#7216CA) serves as a secondary accent, analogous to the primary color, providing depth and contrast for subtle highlights and interactive states, maintaining the overall mystical, romantic gradient.
- The 'Poppins' font is chosen for both headlines and body text to convey a contemporary yet elegant aesthetic, matching the site's romantic and premium feel.
- Utilize a consistent set of delicate, outlined heart icons throughout the application, alongside complementary symbolic icons for buttons (e.g., gift box, camera, confetti) that maintain a soft, romantic style.
- The design prioritizes spacious, clean layouts with a focus on full-screen experiences, particularly the hero section, to create an immersive feel. Generous padding and margins ensure visual breathing room, reinforcing the premium aesthetic on all devices.
- Subtle, smooth animations, including a continuous pink-purple background gradient, gentle floating heart/particle effects, and carefully timed fade-ins and transitions, will create an emotional and magical 'love story experience' without being distracting. Interactive elements like card flips and image hovers will also have fluid, elegant animations.
